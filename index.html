Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of clients do you work with?+
We work with founders, CEOs, executives, consultants, coaches, clinic owners, real estate professionals, service businesses, and SMEs — primarily in Kenya and East Africa, with select international engagements.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes. While our primary market is Kenya and East Africa, we work with international clients remotely. All consultations are conducted virtually across time zones.
Are consultations paid? Can fees be credited?+
Yes, all consultations are paid sessions. All consultation fees can be credited in full toward any implementation package, retainer, or ongoing engagement when you decide to proceed.
Do you offer one-off projects or only retainers?+
Both. We offer one-off project engagements (AI setup, voice agent implementation, SOP documentation) as well as ongoing monthly executive support retainers.
Can you help my business identify practical AI opportunities?+
Absolutely — that's one of our core specialties. Our AI Productivity Consultation includes a workflow audit that maps where AI can save your business time and money.
What happens after the consultation?+
After your consultation, you'll receive a clear summary of recommendations and proposed next steps. If you proceed, your consultation fee is credited in full.
